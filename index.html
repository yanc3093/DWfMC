Data Warehouse for MonCinema Company

About this project

This project was a group school assignment completed in September 2021.

We were provided with an operational database that stores and maintains all of the business transaction information required for the daily operation of a cinema company in an Oracle account. The objective of this project was to design and create a data warehouse that could be used to answer a series of online analytical processing (OLAP) queries.

Project outcomes

This project was executed in 3 stages:

  • Explore the operational database and clean the data.
  • Design and implement the data warehouse represented by 2 versions of star schema diagrams.
  • Generate Business Intelligence (BI) reports based on the Online Analytical Processing (OLAP) queries.

In the first stage, I was responsible for producing the Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) to represent the structure of the operational database.

In the second stage, my teammates and I produced the 2 versions of star schema diagrams together.

  • Version 1 has a high level of aggregation and a low level of granularity.
  • Version 2 has no aggregation and the highest level of granularity.
